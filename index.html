DataOnFocus

Information is one of the most important resources in these days. Our organization focus on every subject related with data. So, in our articles and resources, we try to approach every action related with information, such:

  • Analysis
  • Transformation
  • Normalization
  • Storage

Therefore, our main areas of action are data mining, data warehousing, business intelligence, big data and predictive analytics. To support our work we supply these repositories in order to enrich our resources and what we provide to you with programming examples and projects. The repository is here.

Data Warehousing

Our first explored subject is data warehousing and we developed a couple of articles that covers it's basics. We have an amazing and extensive article about the differences of OLTP and OLAP systems, explaining in detail what is every single one of these.

Please read it and send us your feedback! Other resources will be added when we upgrade our knowledge base.
